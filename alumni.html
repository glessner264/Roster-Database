Alumni

Welcome to the alumni page. Here you can read about Big Spring Alumni who went on to play at the collegiate level.

Matthew Gibson

Year of HS Graduation: 2011

Positions Played: RB, LB

Height: 5-11

College Attended: Lock Haven

College Major: Sports Administration

Matt Gibson was a 4 year starter in high school. In his senior season he was first team offense and second team all-defense for the colonial division. Also in his senior year he helped Big Spring win back the LBJ and make the District 3 playoffs for the first time in school history.

Janosch Beauchamp

Year of HS Graduation: 2011

Positions Played: OL, LB

Height: 6-1

College Attended: Lock Haven

College Major: Physical Therapy

Janosch Beauchamp was a 3 year starter in high school. During his junior and senior seasons he was the second highest tackler in the Colonial Division. He was part of the senior class that won the LBJ and made the District 3 playoffs for the first time in school history.
