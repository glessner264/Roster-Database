Announcements


  • A team meeting is scheduled for Tuesday, April 7th during school 4th period. Players interested in playing football this upcoming season should attend. The meeting will occur during "A" lunch and players will eat "B" lunch.


  • Spring Practice week is scheduled from May 20th to May 24th. Practice is from 3:30 to 5:30 PM everyday. Please make sure to bring cleats if you have them.

  • We will be traveling to Mechanicsburg High School on Saturday, May 25th for team competition. We will be working on 7 on 7 competition and Linemen Skills.

Welcome to the online home of the Big Spring Bulldog football team. This website was created to promote Big Spring Bulldog football and help inform its fan base about its tradition on the gridiron. The Bulldogs are excited about the upcoming 2013 football season. We have an amazing group of student athletes and a dedicated new coaching staff under the leadership of Coach Mike Berry. Come out and cheer on your hometown Bulldogs!
